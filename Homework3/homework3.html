Hello guys, this recipe is about poached egg. It is actually raw egg fried in oil. I love eggs, So this recipe is very simple and easy to make. You guys will love it.

Ingredients needed

  • one egg.
  • cooking oil.
  • salt.

Steps to take

  1. First take a frying pan.
  2. Put a little cooking oil in the pan.
  3. Heat up the oil.
  4. Then break and fry the egg in the pan.
  5. Spray some salt in the egg and after 30 sec pull out the egg.
